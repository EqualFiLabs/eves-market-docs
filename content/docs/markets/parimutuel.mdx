## Pool model

Parimutuel markets do not split collateral into complementary CTF inventory. A user buys YES or NO shares, net market collateral is added to the payout pool, and the minted share amount is weighted by the active epoch multiplier.

Winners claim pro-rata against the finalized pool snapshot. INVALID resolution makes both sides claimable against the pool snapshot according to the parimutuel settlement rules.

## Scheduled entry

The canonical creation entry point takes a typed `CreateParimutuelMarketParams` struct, carrying display and external reference metadata like the orderbook path. Shares are not purchasable before `tradingStartTime`.

```solidity createParimutuelMarket
createParimutuelMarket(CreateParimutuelMarketParams params)
// legacy positional overload also exists for backwards compatibility
```

Profile-backed parimutuel markets use `createParimutuelMarketWithCollateralProfile`. The profile controls the market collateral, payout unit, creation seed amount, and minimum entry for that market.

## Epoch windows

The epoch multiplier schedule starts from `tradingStartTime`, not market creation. `epochWindow` is customized per market, but it must be greater than zero, no longer than the market duration, and no longer than `parimutuelEpochWindowCap`.

This means a 30-day market can use a one-day epoch window if the protocol cap allows it. The early-conviction multiplier then decays across the first day rather than the full 30 days.

## Entry flow

The entry amount must be at least the configured minimum for the market collateral. The protocol deducts the parimutuel entry fee, routes the creator, protocol, seniorPool, resolver, and etRisk fee shares, adds net collateral to the payout pool, and mints weighted ERC1155 shares.

:::note Fee legs
The parimutuel fee split is five-way: creator, protocol, seniorPool, resolver, and etRisk. The seniorPool leg lands in SeniorCapitalPool and the etRisk leg in EtRiskStakingRewards, each with a treasury fallback.
:::

`previewParimutuelEntry` returns the fee breakdown including the etRisk fee, net collateral, shares minted, active epoch, multiplier, effective basis, and projected pool totals. It lives on the parimutuel view facet, separate from the write facet. Frontends should preview before submitting and enforce `minSharesOut`.

## Read surface

Parimutuel reads live on `ParimutuelViewFacet`, separate from the write facet. Preview, payout, pool balances, epoch window, and multiplier reads all resolve there, while create, buy, claim, and dust sweep stay on `ParimutuelFacet`.

## Secondary market

Parimutuel share tokens are ERC1155 balances and can be moved with standard approvals. A secondary CLOB path can exist when makers post inventory, but applications should not assume automatic liquidity or immediate book materialization.

Parimutuel shares do not participate in combinatorial positions. Combo markets build on CTF and native positions, not pooled parimutuel shares.
