## Redemption

Settlement is market-type aware. Orderbook CTF positions redeem through ConditionalTokens payout vectors. Parimutuel positions claim against a finalized pool snapshot.

Before resolution, matching CTF YES and NO balances can merge back into collateral. After resolution, holders should follow the winning-side redemption path.

## Fee configurations

Fees are separated by market surface. Do not use legacy shared names or describe all markets as having the same creator fee path.

| Surface | Fee config |
| --- | --- |
| Orderbook | `orderbookEntryFeeBps`, maker, creator, protocol, and vault split. |
| Spot | `spotTradeFeeBps`, maker, protocol, and vault split. No creator fee. |
| Parimutuel | `parimutuelEntryFeeBps`, creator, protocol, and vault split. |

## Spot fee naming

Spot uses trade fees. Avoid `SPOT_ENTRY_FEE_BPS` in docs and deploy guides. Use `SPOT_TRADE_FEE_BPS`.

The internal book fee struct can map spot trade fee into a shared `entryFeeBps` field for execution accounting, but that is an implementation detail. Product and deployment docs should call it a spot trade fee.

## Fee claiming

Makers can claim maker fees where the CLOB accrues them. Eligible creators can claim creator fees for markets that support creator fees and satisfy the resolution eligibility rules.

Spot books do not have a creator fee in the current protocol.
