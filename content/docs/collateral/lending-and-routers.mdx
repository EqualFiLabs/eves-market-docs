## sEVEUSD lending

The lending layer allows borrowers to use sEVEUSD shares as collateral. Borrow limits, durations, grace periods, fees, and route permissions are governed by protocol parameters.

The key product behavior is that borrowing unlocks usable eveUSD liquidity without requiring the user to fully exit the vault position.

## Routers

Routers package common multi-step flows:

- USDC to eveUSD wrapping.
- USDC to YES and NO split inventory.
- Buying or selling market positions with routed collateral.
- Vault deposit, redeem, wrap, and unwrap flows.
- Maker lending routes for borrow, position creation, and unwind flows.

Routers should enforce residual balance checks and explicit slippage bounds.
