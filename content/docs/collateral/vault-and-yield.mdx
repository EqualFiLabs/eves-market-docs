## Why the vault matters

The protocol separates execution capital from productive capital. eveUSD is the stable market collateral rail, while sEVEUSD gives users a place for idle balances that are not currently posted in markets or maker inventory.

This lets users keep protocol-native capital inside the system without requiring every dollar to sit in an active trade.

## Revenue flow

A portion of protocol revenue can be directed to the vault when the configured staking vault and reward token are active. Vault-directed fees are distinct from protocol treasury fees and market creator fees.

| Flow | Recipient |
| --- | --- |
| Vault fee split | Registered reward flow for sEVEUSD holders. |
| Protocol fee split | Treasury. |
| AUM fee | Treasury capture, not a vault-holder reward. |

## Multi-token rewards

The vault can support registered reward tokens beyond eveUSD. A single sEVEUSD position can accrue whatever reward tokens the vault has enabled and received.

Integrations should query vault reward state rather than assuming every reward is denominated in eveUSD.
