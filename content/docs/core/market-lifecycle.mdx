## State flow

Markets now have a start time and an expiry time. Creation can happen before trading begins, which lets creators prepare future markets in batches without opening them immediately.

| Stage | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Scheduled | The market exists, but `block.timestamp` is before `tradingStartTime`. |
| Trading | Trading or parimutuel entry is open between `tradingStartTime` and `expiryTime`. |
| Expired | New exposure is closed and the market is waiting for resolution. |
| Resolution | Creator settlement, disputes, escalation, or voting establish the final outcome. |
| Settlement | Winning CTF positions redeem or parimutuel shares claim against the finalized pool snapshot. |

Orderbook curves and combo books require started markets. Parimutuel entries also require the market to have reached `tradingStartTime`.

## Scheduled creation

`createMarket` and `createParimutuelMarket` both accept `tradingStartTime` and `expiryTime`. `createMarkets` accepts an array of scheduled orderbook market creation params, capped by `marketCreationBatchCap`.

A market can be created for the future, appear in discovery, and still reject entry or execution until it starts. This is useful for timeboxed markets such as hourly BTC direction, sports slates, or event windows.

:::success Scheduling use case
A creator can prepare 24 one-hour markets in one or more batches, then only manage resolution after each market expires.
:::

## Orderbook vs parimutuel lifecycle

| Topic | Orderbook | Parimutuel |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Position creation | Split eveUSD into complementary CTF YES and NO. | Buy weighted YES or NO pool shares. |
| Trading start | Curves and fills execute only once market execution is open. | Entries are blocked until `tradingStartTime`. |
| Time weighting | CLOB price comes from maker curves. | Epoch multiplier is based on elapsed time since `tradingStartTime`. |
| Settlement | CTF payout vector determines redemption. | Finalized pool snapshot determines claims. |

## Expired market display

Applications should treat expired markets as a separate discovery state. They are not active on the All trading page, but they remain important for resolution, claims, charts, and history.
